# 1. Product Background

In recent years, grade 12 students in Vietnam have struggled a lot when preparing for the National High School Graduation Exam in English. Every night, they spend hours solving practice tests from books or social media. After checking the answer key, they only know their score but do not understand why they made mistakes. They cannot tell if they lack vocabulary, grammar, or have fallen into a reading trap. This breaks their learning momentum, leaving them to wait days to ask a teacher or search helplessly online.

As a result, students waste a lot of time and effort. Because they do not know their exact weaknesses, they keep studying topics they already know, while leaving their real knowledge gaps empty. This blind practice makes them frustrated and want to give up. In a fierce university entrance race where fractions of a point decide everything, a single uncorrected mistake can change a student’s entire future.

This is the perfect time for a solution. Today's English exam requires logical tactics, not just memorisation. Modern students also expect instant feedback on their phones or computers. However, traditional online courses are too passive, forcing everyone through the same linear path. Meanwhile, general AI tools do not understand the local exam matrix. Students urgently need a smart system that aligns perfectly with the exam to guide them effectively.

**Section Summary — Unmet Need**


*While existing educational platforms offer extensive mock exam repositories and pre-recorded lectures, they primarily provide a one-way learning experience and fail to diagnose the exact root causes of student mistakes. Currently, no solution successfully integrates structured, exam-aligned learning paths with a contextual AI tutor capable of instantly pinpointing knowledge gaps and offering personalized remediation. This unmet need for an interactive, automated, and highly targeted exam preparation ecosystem is the core opportunity addressed by the HanGo platform.*


# 3. Proposed Solution

**Proposed Solution**

HanGo is designed to address the major challenges faced by Vietnamese high school students during English National High School Graduation Exam preparation. Instead of relying on fragmented learning resources, disconnected practice websites, and manual self-assessment, the platform provides an integrated environment that combines structured learning, standardized exam practice, performance analysis, and AI-assisted learning support within a single system.

For learners, HanGo offers a comprehensive preparation journey centered around official exam formats. Students can take standardized mock exams, receive immediate results and detailed explanations, identify weaknesses across specific Skill Types and Group Types, and access recommended learning resources to strengthen deficient areas. In addition, learners can study through structured courses, track their learning progress, build personal vocabulary collections, and interact with an AI Learning Assistant that understands the context of the current course and lesson. The assistant helps explain concepts, answer learning-related questions, generate similar examples, and provide personalized study guidance based on the learner’s progress and performance history.

For educational content teams, HanGo provides a centralized platform for managing courses, lessons, examinations, and learning materials. Through a structured workflow, Trainers can develop learning content while Training Leads review, approve, and publish educational resources to ensure quality and consistency. Task assignment and review processes further support efficient collaboration and content governance.

The platform is particularly valuable in the current educational landscape, where demand for online exam preparation continues to grow while many existing solutions remain focused on either static learning materials or standalone testing systems. HanGo bridges the gap between learning and assessment by connecting exam performance with targeted learning resources and AI-assisted support. By combining structured courses, exam analytics, guided recommendations, and contextual learning assistance, the platform aims to improve learning efficiency and help students prepare more effectively for the English National High School Graduation Examination.

# 5. Expected Contributions & Next Steps


## 5.1  Business & Operational Impact 

HanGo centralizes exam practice, course learning, educational content management, learner analytics, and content production workflows within a single platform. By providing a unified environment for Trainer, Training Lead and Learner, the system reduces operational fragmentation and improves collaboration efficiency throughout the content development lifecycle.

For content teams, the task assignment and review workflow enables Training Lead to manage content production systematically while allowing Trainer to track assigned tasks, submit deliverables, and receive review feedback within the same platform. This structured workflow improves transparency, enhances content governance, and reduces the reliance on external communication and tracking tools.

For learners, automated weakness analysis helps transform examination results into actionable learning insights. Instead of manually reviewing dozens of questions to identify weak areas, learners receive structured feedback based on SkillType and GroupType performance. This reduces the effort required to identify learning priorities and enables learners to focus on the topics that need improvement.

The platform also establishes a foundation for scalable educational content management. Courses, lessons, examinations, question banks, and learner analytics are managed through standardized workflows, making it easier to maintain content quality and support future platform growth.


## 5.2  User Experience Impact 

Learners gain a clear, personalised learning path, reducing the frustration and cognitive load of aimless exam preparation. Students benefit from instant, targeted support — automated weakness analysis and a 24/7 contextual AI tutor replace the current practice of only receiving raw scores and struggling alone to figure out why an answer is wrong.  

Trainers gain a streamlined content generation process, reducing the time and effort spent on repetitive manual drafting. Creators benefit from faster, consistent lesson building — AI-automated quiz and flashcard generation based on uploaded materials replaces the current practice of manually typing out every single practice question and its detailed explanation.  

Training Leads gain a single source of truth for all academic content and task progress, reducing the cognitive load of monitoring multiple tracking spreadsheets or chat apps simultaneously. Managers benefit from a seamless quality control process — centralised task assignments and direct approve/reject workflows replace the current practice of scattered communication and manual content verification before publishing.


## 5.3  Planned Next Steps After Project Completion 

 	Following the successful completion and validation of the initial platform version, several future development directions are planned to further expand both educational value and operational capability. Future enhancements may include extending the platform beyond English exam preparation to support additional high school subjects and other standardized examinations, enabling HanGo to evolve into a broader personalised learning ecosystem for secondary education.

Additional future improvements include integrating AI-driven analytics to predict exam performance. Further expansions may also include realistic online exam environments with anti-cheating monitoring mechanisms to better simulate real examination conditions and gamified competitive learning activities based on real user feedback and operational needs.

Overall, the project establishes a practical foundation for future innovation in online exam preparation by emphasising personalised learning support, operational efficiency, and scalable educational management.
